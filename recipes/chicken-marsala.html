Chicken Marsala

Home

Description

This is some classic timeless chicken marsala. You're going to be the talk of the town if you can whip this bad boy up and if you aren't... feel free to sue me! Please, actually sue me. I have too much money.

Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flour 1/4 cup
salt 1/2 teaspoon
ground black pepper 1/4 teaspoon
dried oregano 1/2 teaspoon
chicken breast 4
butter 4 tablespoons
sliced mushrooms 1 cup
marsala wine 1/2 cup
cooking sherry 1/4 cup

Step 1: In a shallow dish or bowl, mix together the flour, salt, pepper and oregano. Coat chicken pieces in flour mixture.

Step 2: In a large skillet, melt butter in oil over medium heat. Place chicken in the pan, and lightly brown. Turn over chicken pieces, and add mushrooms. Pour in wine and sherry. Cover skillet; simmer chicken 10 minutes, turning once, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
